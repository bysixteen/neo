# Alexa Agent Embodiment — Client Design Direction

**Document:** Feedback - Alexa Agent Embodiment
**Model:** Light Membrane Model

---

## Purpose

Evolve Alexa's visual embodiment while preserving continuity with the existing brand language. Alexa has accumulated significant brand recognition over more than a decade — the objective is **evolution, not reinvention**.

Rather than introducing a new visible "assistant object", the design should make Alexa feel like a presence embedded within the interface itself.

---

## Core Concept: Light Membrane

Alexa is represented as a luminous membrane that exists beneath the interface surface.

The UI layer sits above this membrane. When Alexa is active, the UI subtly reveals the presence of this luminous surface beneath it.

The result should feel like light travelling through material, not a glow layered on top of the interface.

### Two Conceptual Layers

| Layer | Role |
|-------|------|
| **Light Membrane (Alexa)** | Continuous luminous surface spanning the display behind the UI. Flexible, elastic, capable of stretching toward areas of interaction. Represents Alexa's presence. |
| **Interface Surface (Neo UI)** | The visible UI layer that sits above the membrane. Soft Neo morphology, graphic clarity, calm visual hierarchy. |

When Alexa is active, the UI surface reveals subtle depth and subsurface translucency as light from the membrane diffuses through it.

---

## Subsurface Scattering

The primary visual mechanism for Alexa's presence is **subsurface scattering**.

Instead of emitting light directly, UI elements should appear to transmit light through their surface. This creates the feeling that the interface is made of a soft translucent material.

**Desired behaviour:**
- Light diffusing beneath a surface
- Illumination emerging through thickness
- Soft gradients that feel volumetric rather than digital

**Material references:**
- Silicone
- Wax
- Frosted polymer
- Light through skin
- Light through thin paper

The effect should feel warm, soft, and physical.

---

## Presence Mode

When Alexa is listening or present:

- The UI layer subtly scales inward, revealing the membrane around the perimeter of the screen
- This creates a frame of luminous presence around the interface
- The effect communicates that Alexa exists **behind** the interface, not above it

---

## Interaction Model

The membrane allows Alexa to interact with the interface from beneath.

- Alexa can stretch the membrane toward regions of the screen
- This causes localised illumination and pressure beneath UI elements

**Uses:**
- Guide attention
- Highlight components
- Indicate interaction targets
- Reinforce conversational responses

Critically, this highlighting occurs **through the surface**, not through overlays or outlines.

---

## Membrane Behaviour

The membrane should behave like a soft elastic material.

**Characteristics:**
- Slow pressure buildup
- Soft deformation
- Smooth release
- Surface tension

Motion should feel calm and organic, not energetic or reactive.

The membrane should **never** behave like liquid or fluid simulation. It should feel closer to a **tensioned elastic surface**.

---

## Motion Principles

Motion should reflect physical material behaviour.

**Important qualities:**
- Smooth acceleration
- Delayed response
- Gentle settling
- Minimal oscillation

**Avoid:**
- Pulses
- Waves
- Scanning animations
- Abrupt morphing

The motion should feel closer to **breathing or pressure shifting beneath a surface**.

---

## Shape Language

The membrane should respect the Neo EDL shape system.

- Pressure from the membrane should produce shapes that feel like Neo forms pushing through the interface surface
- The membrane should **not** introduce unrelated geometry or blob-like forms

---

## Lighting Direction

The lighting should feel natural and warm rather than technological.

**Avoid references that resemble:**
- Plasma
- Neon
- Sci-fi energy
- Electrical effects

**Instead explore light behaviours such as:**
- Diffused illumination
- Caustics
- Subsurface glow
- Ambient reflections
- Light filtered through materials

These qualities evoke **presence and calmness** rather than spectacle.

---

## Environmental Compatibility

The system should function in both light and dark environments.

Because the embodiment relies on light diffusion through surfaces, it should remain legible in daylight UI contexts, not only dark backgrounds.

Avoid designs that rely on strong glow contrast against dark backgrounds.

---

## Desired Emotional Tone

**Alexa should feel:**
- Calm
- Attentive
- Warm
- Quietly intelligent

**The system should not feel:**
- Mechanical
- Flashy
- Energetic
- Theatrical

**The goal is presence, not performance.**

---

## Exploration Focus

Future explorations should prioritise:

1. Membrane deformation behaviours
2. Subsurface light diffusion through UI components
3. Interaction between membrane pressure and Neo shapes
4. Subtle spatial depth within the interface
